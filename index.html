TS3 Reg System

This simple to install and use TS3 registration system, allows your players/users to register with your TS server using their normal credentials. Simple complete the few setup steps and you'll be on your way!

The solution also allows you to develop your own custom authentication method and implement that instead.

NB: The product also comes with a bot, allowing you to setup automatic kicks/bans for prolonged absence/inactivity times.

Setup is simple...

1) Edit includes/config.php to contain your site's details.
2) Configure the authentication method includes/auth/default.php
2a) If using a new/alternative authentication method, be sure to change L29 in config.php
3) Import teamspeak.sql into your database.
4) Enjoy.
